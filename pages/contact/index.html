Contact Us

Who we are and what we do

We are a group of companies, institutes and universities organized as the Modelica Association Project System Structure and Parametrization under the umbrella of the Modelica Association.

We are defining an open standard called System Structure and Parameterization (SSP). This standard will improve and simplify the exchange of complete systems of dynamic models between different modelling, simulation and co-simulation tools. Applications range from software/model/hardware-in-the-loop simulation to embedded systems.

If you are interested in our activities, please contact us: map-ssp_projectlead@googlegroups.com

Project leader: Jochen Köhler
